What we do.

United Nation World Peace Association is involved in
Charity, Events and Volunteer Projects for International
Human Rights Issues, Poverty, Natural Environment,
Health Care, Education and Welfare.

Our mission is to make humanitarian action, World Peace, Promote sustainable
development programs and our Purpose are:

1. ACTIVITIES TO PROMOTE HUMAN RIGHTS ADVOCACY OR PEACE
2. ACTIVITIES TO PROMOTE HEALTH, MEDICAL CARE OR WELFARE
3. ACTIVITIES TO PROMOTE SOCIAL EDUCATION
4. ACTIVITIES TO PROMOTE SCIENCE, CULTURE, ART OR SPORTS
5. ACTIVITIES TO CONSERVE THE ENVIRONMENT
6. DISASTER RELIEF ACTIVITIES
7. INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION ACTIVITIES / INTERNATIONAL ACTIVITIES
8. ACTIVITIES TO PROMOTE THE FORMATION OF GENDER EfUALITY SOCIETY
9. ACTIVITIES TO REVITALIZE ECONOMIC ACTIVITIES.
UNWPA PERFORMS THE FOLLOWING TO ACHIEVE THE PURPOSE

1. COUNSELING FOR THE PROTECTION OF HUMAN RIGHTS.
2. PROMOTIOM OF HEALTH, MEDICAL CARE OR WELFARE, VOLUNTEER SUCH AS DISASTER RELIEF AND HEALTH PROMOTION.
3. EDUCATION AND SEMINARS, CONFERENCE/ MEETING TO PROMOTE SOCIAL EDUCATION.
4. BUSINESS THAT CONTRIBUTE TO THE PROTECTION AND DEVELOPMENT OF HISTORY AND HERITAGE.
5. BUSINESS FOR CLEANING AND REPLANTING FOR ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION.
6. VOLUNTEER PROJECT IN INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION.
7. BUSINESS MATCHING FOR ACTIVITIES OF ECONOMIC ACTIVITY.

The sustainable Development Goals are the blueprint to achieve a better and more
sustainable future for all.

They address the global challenge we face, including those related to poverty, inequality,
climate change, environmental degradation, peace and justice.
