Tree Plantation ( The World Environmental Day)

Today 10th June 2021, in the supervision of UNWPA Bangladesh President H. E. Ratan Kumar Das , UNWPA Bangladesh has arranged a day long tree plantation program on the occasion of ” The World Environmental Day”. Today some Ambassadors of UNWPA Bangladesh chapter’s has plant some trees of Middle Badda area in Dhaka. They have planted different types of trees like as Herb tress, fruit trees, flowering plants etc.

H. E. Firoz Alam Sumon , Ambassador & Director (Bangladesh), H. E. Syed Azmul Haque, Ambassador & Representative for Operation Management (Bangladesh), H. E. Ariful Islam Razib, Ambassador & Representative for Foreign Affairs (Bangladesh), H. E. Md Alamgir Hossain, Ambassador & Director for Planning Activities (Bangladesh), H. E Lovely Bandana, Ambassador & Representative for councilor officer ( Bangladesh ), H. E Mitaly Rani Karmakar, Representative of Poverty and Medical ( Bangladesh ), H. E. Md Iyasir Arafat, Ambassador & Representatives for Environment and Disaster (Bangladesh). and H. E. Mohammad Uzzal Hossain, Ambassador & Representatives for Science and Technology (Bangladesh), these honorable Ambassadors have attended thisTree plantation program respectively.

We wish everyone protection in this Covid-19 epidemic. Please stay safe, stay Home.
